Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Zoom account?

Click the "Connect Zoom" button in the app. You will be redirected to Zoom's authorization page where you can grant the app access to your meeting data.

How do I connect my Google Calendar?

Click the "Connect Google Calendar" button. You will be redirected to Google's authorization page to grant calendar access.

How do I revoke access?

  • Zoom: Go to Zoom App Marketplace > Manage > Installed Apps > Remove Interview Agent
  • Google: Go to Google Account > Security > Third-party apps > Remove Interview Agent

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use OAuth 2.0 for authentication, store tokens in an encrypted database, and never log sensitive data in plaintext. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
