Getting Started

Interview Agent syncs your Zoom meetings with Google Calendar. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Install the app from Zoom App Marketplace
  2. Authorize the app to access your Zoom account
  3. Connect your Google Calendar account
  4. Your meetings will automatically sync between Zoom and Google Calendar

How It Works

Meeting Sync

When a meeting is created, updated, or deleted in Zoom, the app automatically reflects those changes in your Google Calendar. Sync happens in real time via Zoom webhooks.

Supported Events

  • Meeting Created: A new Google Calendar event is created with Zoom meeting details
  • Meeting Updated: The corresponding calendar event is updated to reflect changes
  • Meeting Deleted: The calendar event is automatically removed

Authentication

The app uses OAuth 2.0 for both Zoom and Google Calendar. Your access tokens are securely stored and automatically refreshed so you stay connected without re-authenticating.

Removing the App

  1. Go to Zoom App Marketplace
  2. Click Manage > Installed Apps
  3. Find "Interview Agent" and click Uninstall
  4. Optionally, revoke Google Calendar access from your Google Account settings

All your data and tokens will be removed from our systems upon uninstallation.
